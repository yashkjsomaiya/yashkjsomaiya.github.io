Nihar Hodawadekar (born 23 July 1999) also widely known as Nazz is an Indian rapper from the suburbs of Mumbai, Maharashtra called Virar. Nazz started his career in music as a playback singer but with time he discovered Rap and Hip-hop culture and switched to Rap. He is active in the Underground Hip-hop since 2018, he blew up in the mainstream after he participated in the Indian national rap show MTV Hustle 2.0. He creates is music across various sub-genres of Hihop and is a prominent veteran of Mumbai Underground Hip-hop scene.

Childhood

Nihar Hodawadekar was born on 23rd July 1999 in Mumbai. He initially was brought up in BIT Chawl, Mazgaon, Mumbai but then later shifted to Virar to live with his uncle's family. His uncle and aunty raised him from thereon. He has had a very tough childhood and has experienced a lot of emotionally disturbing events that shaped him throughout childhood. He has been open about this in his songs but he refuses to address it freely in the interviews. As far as the information stretches, 'his mother left home to never return when he was one year old' he has mentioned in a song named RIP. 'His father was accused of fraud and he died when Nazz was 16 years old' he said in his songs. In his early youth he got into wrong company and he also mentions doing robbery in his own home. Although later on he will decide to improve as Hip-hop enters his life.

Early days

Nihar in his early days used to make covers of Bollywood songs and upload on his youtube channel and was trying to become an upcoming playback singer in the Indian pop genre. He also has a original song released with T-series. While he was aware of the Rap songs and the term Hip-hop. After a few years in his singing career he started to explore the Hip-hop culture and finally switched his genre and became a full time rapper. He explored and got inspired by both Indian (desi) as well as American and other Rappers.

He released his music on Youtube and his first few songs consisted of collabs which ranged from his close friend (turned rapper) Madx to Mumbai Underground rapper Shaikhspeare. The collabs helped him to gain some audience to start with and then he maintained a streak of uploading music on Youtube and various other music streaming platforms. After releasing a few tracks on his channel, no proper response from the audience was visible until he dropped a Gangsta/Banger track Naake pe Aake dekh which blew up and many in the Indian Hiphop community started getting familiar with his name. After this he started gaining traction in the Indian Hip-hop scene and he consistently dropped high performing tracks which ranged from Gangsta to Emotional to Motivational.

Almost Mainstream

In September of 2020, Nazz released a single Leke Chal Mujhe which was his narration about his dark past related to his parents. He also talked about his Satanic inclination and how God has not contributed to anything in his life. This song blew among underground Desi Hip-Hop audience and he started to gain traction in the scene. After this track a few more singles were dropped by him until his big project Orphan EP was released on Youtube. The first track in the EP - the title track itself was shared by Raftaar a Desi hip-hop veteran, this gave this EP and Nazz a major push. While one more track - Heavy Driver from this 3 song EP blew up and it also has significance in his future undertakings. After this Nazz kept on releasing music consistently and many more songs among these blew up, some being Maut ka Kuan, Bakasur and Agar Mein Hota Bhagwaan. Nazz's risk apetite has not been affected by the success and he has experimented with his sounds and has taken himself to various sub-genres of Hip-hop, While all this happens, he has got a music producer - Audiocrackerr, who is there to stay with him for a long run. While all this hustle was happening, a major catalyst was on it's way to accelearate Nazz's progress.

MTV Hustle 2.0

Nazz participated as a contestant in the Rap Reality show MTV Hustle Season 2 or MTV Hustle 2.0. He was one of the most experienced consestant in the show along with the likes of GD47 and Gravity. While all of his tracks were loved by Audience. He sprinted to the finals of the show with 2 performer of the week titles. Earning a lot of appreciation from the Judge Badshah. He ended up being the 2nd runner in the show. This show helped Nazz to be exposed to a grater audience and increased his fan following exponentially. Nazz started this show as a jolly rapper but by the end of the show, he had delivered emotional and hardcore tracks too. MTV Hustle played a role in Nazz's journey and was a cataylst in his success.
